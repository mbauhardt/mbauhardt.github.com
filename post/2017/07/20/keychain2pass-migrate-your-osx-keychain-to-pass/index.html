Keychain2Pass - Migrate Your OSX Keychain To Pass

July 20, 2017

In my previous blog post wrote about a better GPG keychain. I’m using a yubikey to encrypt/decrypt documents.

Why not using the yubikey key for my passwords too? So i decided to store my passwords no more in my OSX keychain. I wanted to switch from OSX keychain to pass.

It turns out that the manual migration from the OSX keychain to pass is really time consuming. I was to lazy to it manually. For that reason i wrote a small programm to to this job.

Give keychain2pass a try if you also want to switch from the OSX keychain to pass.
